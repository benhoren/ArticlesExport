Middlesbrough Ramsdens pawnbroker listed on stock market
A Middlesbrough pawnbroker has become the town's only business to be listed on the stock market.
Ramsdens opened a single shop in neighbouring Stockton thirty years ago and is now worth more than £25m.It has become well known as Middlesbrough Football Club's shirt sponsor.Chief executive Peter Kenyon said there was still a stigma attached to pawnbroking but only among "people who don't understand it".Three quarters of Ramsdens' business is now in foreign exchange. It changed more than £300m last year.
